WHO WE ARE

OUR IDENTITY

We are qualified professionals, horse lovers, with experience in different sectors.

We aim to spread our specialist knowledge in the equestrian world beyond may be done without adequate risk management.

We therefore support the Customer in the most disparate contexts of this world: when he lives his life equestrian passion, in its related businesses and, finally, in the protection of those extraordinary creatures that are horses.

OUR VALUES

We strive every day to guarantee protection and safety in the equestrian world, following fundamental principles that guide our work.

Protection

We put protection at the center of all our actions. Whether it's protecting horses, riders or investments, safety is always our top priority.

  • Personalized insurance solutions
  • Preventive risk strategies
  • Ongoing customer support

Competence

Our experience in the equestrian industry is combined with financial, legal and expertise high level insurance companies, offering a truly complete service.

  • Qualified multidisciplinary team
  • Continuous training and updating
  • In-depth knowledge of the sector

Passion

The passion for horses and the equestrian world guides our every decision. We understand profoundly the special bond between riders and horses.

  • Authentic love for the equestrian world
  • Understanding specific needs
  • Dedication to animal safety

OUR MISSION

Our goal is to create a safer and more secure environment for everyone who works in the equestrian world, from amateur riders to professionals, from horse owners to equestrian facilities.

Through an innovative and personalized approach to risk management, we aim to:

  • Reduce the risks associated with equestrian activities
  • Protect the well-being of horses and their owners
  • Offer tailor-made financial and legal solutions
  • Promote a culture of prevention in the sector

WHERE WE ARE

Our main office is located in Florence, but we are operational and provide assistance throughout Europe, the Middle East and North Africa. Contact us to find out how we can help you.
