FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We answer the most common questions about our financial services

To apply for a mortgage loan you generally need:

  • Identity document and tax code
  • Income documentation (latest tax returns)
  • Documentation relating to the property to be purchased
  • Any documents relating to other ongoing financing

Our team will assist you in preparing all the necessary documentation.

Current account agreements are agreements stipulated with banking institutions that allow our members to access advantageous conditions. These benefits may include:

  • Reduction or elimination of the monthly fee
  • Free or reduced-cost operations
  • More favorable interest rates
  • Additional services included

To access these agreements it is sufficient to be associated with Equestrian World Protection or one of the affiliated bodies.

The time it takes to obtain a surety bond can vary based on several factors, including:

  • The completeness of the documentation provided
  • The amount of collateral required
  • The complexity of the operation

On average, with complete documentation, times vary from 5 to 15 working days. Our team works to speed up the process and ensure quick response times.
