THE EQUESTRIAN WORLD AND ITS RISKS

The horse is a majestic animal, endowed with extraordinary physical strength. This aspect determines sometimes practical implications that are not always positive.

For example, to ward off a bothersome insect, the horse could, even without intending to, injure it with a kick, even lethally, a human being. Even falls, regardless of amateur or nature professional riding carried out, can lead to serious injuries.

Furthermore, a possible escape onto the road could cause damage to things and/or people, with consequent compensation obligations for the person. Finally, it is no coincidence that horse riding is considered by our jurisprudence to be a potentially dangerous sporting activity.

As highlighted above, the love for these splendid animals therefore entails a whole series of related risks to which we will inform you and from which we intend to protect you through the collaboration and support of figures different professionals, in the insurance, legal, accounting and veterinary fields.
