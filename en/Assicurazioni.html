HORSES INSURANCE

We insure equines, from their conception, without value limits and regardless of their use.

We offer specific insurance for your horses which mainly covers the CASE OF DEATH DUE TO INJURY and/or ILLNESS, with extension to cases of VANDAL ACT, ACCIDENTAL POISONING and KILLING FOR HUMANITARIAN REASONS, in many countries, both during the journey and during the stay in these States.

This service is aimed at all categories of horses:

  • Horse racing competition
  • Riding outfit
  • Trot
  • Gallop
  • Obstacle races
  • Endurance
  • Dressage
All age groups
  • Foal foals
  • Foals yearlings
  • Competition horses
  • Stallions and mares

Warranty extensions are also offered to the basic policy, for a limited premium authorization from the Company, which guarantee the Customer even more specific and complete coverage.

The insurance guarantee is extended to the case of death of the insured horse following surgical interventions even under total anesthesia, including cases in which they are not necessary to safeguard the life of the horse.

The insurance guarantee extends to medical expenses incurred by the Contractor for surgical operations under general anesthesia and related veterinary fees, hospitalization expenses, pharmaceutical and rehabilitation therapies.

This guarantee is subject to limits per event and per insurance year agreed from time to time with the Company and is usually granted in common risk with all policies taken out in favor of the same policyholder.

The insurance guarantee is intended to be extended to medical expenses incurred by the Contractor for hospitalizations in veterinary clinics following an accident and/or illness relating to veterinary fees, hospitalization expenses, pharmaceutical expenses, rehabilitation therapies and anything else relating to the aforementioned situations limited to the period of hospitalization itself.

This guarantee is subject to limits per event and per insurance year.

La garanzia assicurativa si intende estesa ai casi di:
  • Theft of insured horses
  • Death that is a direct consequence of the theft of the same

The guarantee is extended if the insured person manifests a clinical condition during the period of validity of the policy, based on the diagnosis of the policyholder's veterinarian, confirmed by the Company's trusted veterinarian, which, although not requiring culling for humanitarian reasons, renders the horse permanently incapable of carrying out the functions for which it is insured.

The insurance guarantee covers any loss suffered by the Contractor in the event that the insured stallion becomes permanently impotent, sterile or unfit for breeding as a direct consequence of injury or illness occurring during the contract.

OTHER COVERAGES AVAILABLE

Horse Civil Liability

Guarantee for damage that the animal causes to third parties, even during events. Not all head of household policies include this hypothesis and leave it to the policy of the center or whoever organizes the event.

This involves taking over the compensation obligation of the person who owns or has possession of the animal at the time of the damaging event.

Equestrian Centers

We offer business liability and fire damage coverage for equestrian centers, sports associations and equestrian facilities, with customized solutions.

  • RC coverage Activities: For damage involuntarily caused to third parties

Demonstrations and Events
