OUR OPERATIONAL METHOD

The establishment and maintenance of the professional relationship with the customers are explained in the following operational phases:

PHASE 1

RISK AND INSURANCE PORTFOLIO ANALYSIS IN BE

Together with the Client, also with possible external support from risk managers, evaluation and preventive estimate company, specialized experts and lawyers, we proceed with an in-depth analysis of the company's characteristic activity, or professional activities performed, to identify risk areas existing.

This study is the basis for then analyzing the contracts in detail existing insurance policies, with particular attention to the guarantees and clauses that compose them, to optimize everything, also the economic aspect of each policy.

PHASE 2

DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROGRAM INSURANCE
  • Preparation of a program insurance aimed at optimizing the degree of coverage provided for each policy, in relation to the legal profile and economical, obtained through collaboration with the best Italian and foreign companies.
  • Analyses, assessment And decision, in agreement with the Customer, of the methods of implementation of the proposed insurance solution.
  • Management of the contracts insurance and of any accidents, even if they have not reached a definition.
  • Processing periodical of reports synthetic on the state of the insurance portfolio.
  • Constant monitoring of insurance contracts. There let's update because they have content over time always current, based on the development of guaranteed activities, as risks increase or decrease and as the legislative provisions.

PHASE 3

CLAIMS MANAGEMENT

The occurrence of the accident and its management up to the settlement represents the moment of verify of effectiveness and efficiency of the insurance solutions studied.

Such is the importance of this phase that we have reserved an office, with a lawyer and specialized personnel, committed every day to managing relationships between clients and lawyers, liquidators, experts involved in the process of compensation for damages.

IMPORTANCE OF PREVENTION

As you can easily understand, the role of insurance is to pay damages when you verify, it is crucial.

But it is equally important to also aim to prevent or at least reduce risks.

Avoiding harmful events, in fact, allows more peaceful activities for operators in the sector and others those who practice horseback riding, for example, not falling into long and complex legal cases, through protocols and practices management about which we will keep you informed.
