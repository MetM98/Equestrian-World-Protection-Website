1. WHAT IS A COOKIE?

Cookies are small text files sent from a website or mobile application to your computer, tablet or mobile device. Usually, a cookie contains the name of the website from which the cookie originates, the "duration" of the cookie (i.e., how long it endures on the User's device), and a value, which is usually a randomly generated unique number, but does not contain any personal information such as name or email address. It facilitates navigation from page to page of a website, provides secure connections and, when you return, remembers the preferences expressed during the previous visit. With the information received through this technology, EQUESTRIAN WORLD PROTECTION intents to improve the User’s experience on its website.

2. TYPES OF COOKIES

Based on the characteristics and use of cookies, we can distinguish different categories:

Strictly necessary 'technical' cookies. These are cookies that are essential for the correct functioning of the Website. This category also includes analytics cookies, when used directly by the Website operator to collect, for statistical purposes, anonymous and aggregate information on the number of visitors to the Website and on how they visit the site itself. Disabling technical cookies could compromise the use of the Website. The prior consent of the user is not required for the use of these cookies.

Profiling cookies. These are permanent cookies used to identify (anonymously or not) user preferences and improve their browsing experience. The website uses this type of cookie. For the use of these cookies, the user's prior consent is required.CONSENT FOR THE INSTALLATION OF PROFILING COOKIES
